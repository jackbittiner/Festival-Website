Emmy Lahouel
producing the Fantasy space.

Choreographer and Mixed Art-form producer based in Manchester, she produces and curates Meraki Sessions (Currently at the Wonder Inn), an interdisciplinary event promoting Manchester based artists supported by Dance Manchester. She is an associate artist for “The Welcoming Party” with MIF, as well as Artist programmer and Liaison for HOME’s event How to be Human coming this august. She performed at many platform events such as Turn 2016/17, The Lowry’ Shoots scratch night as well as smaller venues across the north west; her most recent work InMotion commissioned by HOMEmcr is to be performed in august this year.

Isaac Rose
producing the Fairytale space.

Isaac is a programmer and producer living and working in Manchester. Over the past year he has produced two large scale mixed arts events at Islington Mill called RITE. He is part of Contact Theatre's RE:CON programme for young producers and was selected by Manchester International Festival's Creative50 programme. He is also a visual artist working with liquid light displays, and has an upcoming installation at HOME during August 2017.

Marge Girdzijauskaite
producing the Dream space.

Marge has several years of experience on art specific project management and currently is working as part of audiovisual collective MorestateLab. Her most recent productions were showcased at Leeds Light Night 2016, Spectra 2016 (Aberdeen light festival), Liverpool Light Night 2017, Culture Night 2017 (Lithuania). Upcoming performances are Yaga Modern Rituals 2017(Lithuania) and IN-SONORA 2018 Music Festival (Madrid).
